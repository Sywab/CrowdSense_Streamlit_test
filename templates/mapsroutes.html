Alternative Modes of Transportation

When LRT-2 stations are crowded or unavailable, consider these alternative transport options that follow similar routes.

Jeepney

Traditional Filipino public transport, available along major roads parallel to LRT-2.

Route: Recto to Santolan (via Aurora Blvd.)

Frequency: Every 5-10 minutes

Fare Rates: ₱12 - ₱25

UV Express

Air-conditioned vans with fixed routes, offering a faster and more comfortable ride.

Route: Recto to Pasig / Cubao to Antipolo

Frequency: Every 10-15 minutes

Fare Rates: ₱40 - ₱60

Bus

City buses run along Marcos Highway and Aurora Blvd., connecting LRT-2 stations and beyond.

Route: Santolan to Recto (via Marcos Highway / Aurora Blvd.)

Frequency: Every 10-20 minutes

Fare Rates: ₱15 - ₱35
